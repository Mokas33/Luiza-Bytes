Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Luiza Bytes Catering is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect through this website and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The message you send us

We do not collect any other personal information and we do not use cookies or tracking tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit through our contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to any third parties, except Formspree (our form processing service) which facilitates the delivery of your message to us.

3. Formspree

Our contact form is processed by Formspree (formspree.io). Your submission is transmitted securely to Formspree's servers and forwarded directly to us. You can review Formspree's own privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Data Retention

We only retain your contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry. We do not store your data in any database on this website.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request that we delete any personal information you have sent us. To do so, please use the contact form on our website or reach us via WhatsApp on 0723357393.

6. Security

This website is served over HTTPS, meaning all data transmitted between your browser and our site is encrypted. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please use the contact form on our website or reach us on WhatsApp at 0723357393.
